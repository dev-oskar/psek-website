Netlify Form Test

This is a test form to verify Netlify form functionality. It uses the simplest possible implementation to test form submissions.

Debugging Information

If your form isn't working, check the following:

  • Ensure Form Detection is enabled in your Netlify dashboard
  • Verify that the form has the 'netlify' attribute
  • Ensure all field names match between your visible form and the hidden form in public/index.html
  • Check if there are any JavaScript handlers intercepting the form submission
